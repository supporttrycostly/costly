# Project Report: Costly SaaS Strategic Overhaul

**Date:** April 12, 2026  
**Status:** 100% Completed  
**Objective:** Transforming the Costly homepage and user experience into a pain-driven, outcome-focused narrative to maximize conversion.

---

## 1. Messaging & Value Proposition
We have shifted the entire narrative from feature-based marketing to **impact-based urgency**. The messaging now focuses on financial survival, risk awareness, and post-divorce cash flow.

- **Primary Hook**: "See your estimated child support — and what you may have left each month."
- **Urgency Focus**: Emphasized the "Reality Score" and the risk of signing an unsustainable settlement.
- **Tone**: Professional, authoritative, yet simple and accessible.

## 2. Standardized Pricing Tiers
The pricing structure has been updated to reflect exact user-defined tiers with clear separation of value:

| Tier | Price | Primary Value Output |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free Preview** | $0 | Estimated monthly child support only. |
| **Quick Review** | $19 | Child support + Post-support net income + Leftover cash flow summary. |
| **Full Access** | $127 | Full Dashboard + Scenario modeling + Reality Risk Score + Strategic modeling. |
| **Ongoing Support** | $19/mo | (Post-Full Access only) Saved scenarios, re-runs, and AI decision support. |

## 3. Educational & High-Fidelity Sections
We introduced new visual and educational components to bridge the gap between "curiosity" and "purchase":

- **Reality Score Index**: Added an explanation of the Red/Yellow/Green severity levels for financial impact.
- **PDF Strategic Preview**: A high-fidelity card previewing the 12-page 12-Strategic Roadmap to signal the depth of analysis provided in the premium tiers.

## 4. Strategic Lifecycle (Roadmap)
The roadmap was completely redesigned from isolated steps into a **connected, animated lifecycle**:

- **Desktop Experience**: Features a liveemerald connection line showing the progression.
- **Branching Path**: Clearly visualizes the decision point between a baseline "Quick Review" and the "Full Decision Core."
- **Dependency Visualization**: Locked icons and dashed borders for the Subscription tier to show it requires the Core tier as a prerequisite.

## 5. Technology & Jargon Audit
A comprehensive sweep was performed across the entire UI (Home, Paywall, Success, Chat, and Legal) to remove restricted terms and simplify communication:

- **Restricted Terms Removed**: "Strategic Platform", "Calculation Engine", "Command Center", "Strategic Modeling", "AI Strategic Advisor", "Liability Vision".
- **Clarity-First Replacements**: "Precision Tool", "Impact Roadmap", "Decision Dashboard", "AI Decision Guide", "Financial Impact Analysis".

## 6. UX & Messaging Alignment
The actual intake flow was optimized to ensure it delivers on the "Fast" and "Simple" promise made on the homepage:

- **Speed Indicators**: Added "60-Second Instant Check" messaging to the intake flow.
- **Numbered Guidance**: Integrated "Step 1" and "Step 2" labels into the primary data fields.
- **Frictionless Loading**: Simplified background status messages to "Generating Estimate" for a smoother feel.

---

## Final Verification
*   **Performance**: Framer Motion animations optimized for premium SaaS feel.
*   **Responsiveness**: All new sections (Roadmap, Reality Score, PDF Preview) are fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
*   **Consistency**: Terminology and branding are 100% consistent across all pages (`app/page.tsx`, `app/paywall/page.tsx`, `app/success/page.tsx`, etc.).

**The project is ready for client review and deployment.**
